كلمةٌ - Word

Words are broken up into three types

  • اسم - Nouns: Characterized by the following four:
    1. Definite (معرفة) and Indefinite (نكيرة)
      • Definite is either a name or has ال preceding it.
      • Indefinite has tanween
    2. اعراب- The Three States (حال)
      1. رفع
      2. نصب
      3. جرّ
    3. Number
      1. مفرد - Singular
      2. مثنّى - Dual
        • Masculine: Add fatha and انِ
        • Feminine: Remove ة and add تانِ
      3. جمع - Plural
        1. Regular
          • جمع المذكر السالم - Sound Masculine Plurals: Add ونَ
          • جمع المؤنث السالم Sound Feminine Plural: اتِ
        2. Irregular - No set rules
    4. Gender
      • By default, words are masculine
      • Words are classified as feminine if they are naturally feminine or they have
  • فعل - Verbs
  • حرف - Particles
